Welcome to kernelci.org!

Automated Linux Kernel Testing

…made easy!

kernelci.org is a community based, open source distributed test automation system focused on upstream Linux kernel development.
Our goal is to unify all upstream Linux kernel testing efforts in order to provide a single place where to store, view, compare and track these results.

It is our mission to detect, bisect, report and fix regressions on upstream Kernel trees before they even reach «mainline».

For information on how to get your board into kernelci, or get involved with the project, please see the FAQ.
